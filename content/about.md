# About Me

I'm Wouter De Coster, a bioinformatician with a focus on long-read sequencing technologies, particularly Oxford Nanopore sequencing. I work on developing tools and methods for analyzing genomic data.

## What I Do

- Develop bioinformatics tools for long-read sequencing analysis
- Create data visualization solutions for complex genomic data
- Contribute to open-source bioinformatics software
- Share insights and experiences through this blog
- Organize conferences, such as the [Flanders Nanopore Day](https://nanoporeflanders.be/)
