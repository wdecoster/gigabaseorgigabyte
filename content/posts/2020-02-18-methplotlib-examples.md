We recently published [methplotlib](https://academic.oup.com/bioinformatics/advance-article/doi/10.1093/bioinformatics/btaa093/5735409), a tool for the visualization and analysis of modified nucleotides from nanopore sequencing. It works downstream of tools like [nanopolish](https://github.com/jts/nanopolish), [nanocompore](https://github.com/tleonardi/nanocompore) and direct methylation calling by the guppy basecaller. More information can be found on [GitHub](https://github.com/wdecoster/methplotlib). Feedback, suggestions, reporting problems and feature requests are very much appreciated.
